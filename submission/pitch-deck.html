Platform Lock-In

Why Seeker?

TrustTap cannot exist on any other phone.

SGT = $500 cost-of-attack floor

No software wallet or browser extension can replicate a soulbound NFT tied to physical hardware. SGT is worth 25 of 100 trust points, the single largest scoring dimension.

Mobile Wallet Adapter for meeting verification

On-device signing without exposing private keys. QR scan opens MWA, both parties sign, proof goes on-chain via SPL Memo.

SKR-native identity scoring

.skr domains earn higher identity scores than .sol domains, rewarding Seeker-native identity adoption.
